The company

A name that unites two worlds

Relativity and quantum physics are the two great theories that describe the universe: the infinitely large and the infinitely small. While the world waits for a theory of everything to unite them, we chose to unite them ourselves — in our name.

It is how we think about our work: holding together the wider view and the care for detail, the solidity of experience and the curiosity of looking ahead.

Relaquantix S.r.l. was founded in 2024 on its founder's thirty years of experience in online commerce, website and application development, web marketing and publishing. On that foundation we build high-quality digital and editorial products.
