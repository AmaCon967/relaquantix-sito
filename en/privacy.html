Last updated: 16 July 2026

This policy explains how personal data of users visiting relaquantix.com is processed, under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (the “GDPR”) and applicable Italian law.

Types of data processed

Browsing data

The IT systems and software procedures that operate this website acquire, in the course of their normal running, certain data whose transmission is implicit in the use of internet communication protocols (for example IP address, browser and device type, pages visited, date and time of the request). This data is used solely to ensure the correct operation and security of the website, and is processed in aggregate form for technical statistical purposes.

Data provided voluntarily by the user

Sending messages to the email addresses shown on this website involves acquiring the sender's address and any personal data contained in the message, as needed to respond to the request.

Purposes and legal bases

  • Operation and security of the website — legal basis: the controller's legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Responding to user requests — legal basis: response to a request from the data subject and legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR).
  • Compliance with legal obligations — legal basis: legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).

Cookies

This website does not use profiling cookies, nor any third-party analytics or tracking tools. Only technical cookies strictly necessary for the site to function may be present, and these do not require consent. Further details are available in the Legal notice.

Recipients of the data

Data is stored on servers managed by our hosting provider (Hostinger), acting as data processor. Data is not disclosed or sold to third parties for commercial purposes. It may be communicated to public authorities where required by law.

Data transfers

Data is processed within the European Union. Should the hosting provider process data outside the EU, this will take place in compliance with the safeguards set out in the GDPR (for example the standard contractual clauses adopted by the European Commission).

Retention period

Browsing data is kept only for as long as strictly necessary for security and diagnostic purposes. Data contained in email messages is kept for as long as needed to handle the request and to comply with any legal obligations.

Your rights

You may at any time exercise the rights set out in Articles 15 to 22 of the GDPR: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection to processing and data portability, as well as withdrawal of consent where given. To exercise these rights, write to info@relaquantix.com or to the certified address relaquantix@pec.it.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority (in Italy, the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali — garanteprivacy.it).

Children

This website is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Should data relating to a minor be processed without a valid basis, it will be deleted as soon as possible.

Changes

The controller may update this policy. The version published on this page is the one in force; please review it from time to time. The date above indicates the last update.
