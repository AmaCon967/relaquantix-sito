We're here to help.

Do you need help with one of our apps or products? Our team is available. Write to us and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

How to get help faster

To help us solve your problem more quickly, please include where possible:

  • the name of the app or product;
  • the device you are using (for example iPhone, iPad, Android phone) and its operating system version;
  • a description of the problem and, if possible, the steps to reproduce it;
  • any screenshots showing the error.
We answer support requests as quickly as we can, usually within a few working days.
